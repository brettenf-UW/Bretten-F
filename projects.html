High School Scheduling AI Platform

Python MILP AWS AI
Chico High School main campus building
Schedule optimization process workflow diagram
Chico High School logo

Problem

High school scheduling is a manual, months-long process requiring extensive administrator expertise. Current solutions only partially automate this task.

Goal

Create an intelligent system that fully automates schedule creation, reducing the process from months to hours while maximizing course fulfillment.

Actions

  1. Research & Analysis: Collaborated with school administrators to capture expert scheduling strategies and constraints.
  2. System Development: Built hybrid optimization engine using Gurobi solver and Mixed Integer Linear Programming (MILP) with Python for automated conflict resolution.
  3. Solution Deployment: Implemented AWS-hosted solution processing 20+ scheduling constraints for student preferences, teacher assignments, and department requirements.

Outcome

Solution achieves 100% course fulfillment, reduces scheduling time from 3 months to hours, and scales to handle schools with up to 2,000 students.

E-commerce Sales & Shipping Analytics in Power BI

Power BI Data Analysis Cost Optimization
Executive Summary Dashboard showing sales performance and key performance indicators
Shipping Metrics Dashboard analyzing delivery performance and cost-saving opportunities

Problem

Pet supply retailer lacked clear insights into sales performance, product profitability, and shipping costs, hindering data-driven decision making.

Goal

Create PowerBI analytics dashboards to identify purchasing patterns, cross-selling opportunities, and cost-saving strategies for improved profitability.

Actions

  1. Data Analysis: Built unified model from multiple data sources, analyzing 24,000+ transactions across 50 states to reveal regional sales patterns.
  2. Market Analysis: Developed interactive visualizations for regional performance, customer lifetime value, and product category profitability.
  3. Cost Optimization: Created what-if scenarios to model shipping cost reductions through order quantity adjustments.

Outcome

Identified highest-margin product categories and most frequently purchased items. Analysis revealed $118K potential shipping savings through order consolidation. Executive dashboard can assist with data-based decisions on inventory planning, marketing strategy, and logistics optimization.

Finding a Competitive Advantage | Healthcare Trust Case Study

Market Analysis Risk Assessment Strategic Planning
Anonymized client presentation slide showing savings-risk analysis matrix for healthcare solutions
Senate Bill 1067 document limiting what competitor plans pay for hospital services
Map of Oregon counties affected by the healthcare trust services

Problem

A state law gave competitor public employee benefit plans a significant cost advantage by limiting what they pay for hospital services to 200% of Medicare rates (dropping to 190% by 2029). This threatened a regional healthcare trust that provides insurance to cities and counties across the state.

Goal

Analyze benefit plans compared to competitors and develop strategies to maintain competitive position in the public employee insurance market despite the pricing disadvantage.

Actions

  1. Competitor Analysis: Researched public employee benefit plans to compare coverage options, pricing structures, and service offerings against the trust's plans.
  2. Solution Risk Analysis: Worked with industry experts to identify cost-saving healthcare solutions that would maintain quality care while reducing financial burden.
  3. Strategic Recommendations: Presented comprehensive reports to leadership analyzing market position and proposing targeted cost-saving solutions with detailed risk assessments.

Outcome

The healthcare trust determined they would like to pursue a medical marketing strategy and implement the proposed solutions.

Revolutionizing Backcountry Cuisine | Feral Foods

Market Research Product Development Business Planning
Feral Foods brand logo combining outdoor and culinary elements
Front of AdventurePack packaging for Tikka Masala meal
Back of AdventurePack packaging showing nutrition and preparation instructions
2024-2025 Financial forecast projecting growth and profitability
Rehydrated Tikka Masala meal ready to eat in the outdoors

Problem

Backpackers must sacrifice food quality and taste when choosing shelf-stable meals for outdoor adventures. As a Business Honors student, I tackled this problem for my undergraduate thesis at the University of Oregon.

Goal

Develop premium freeze-dried meals that maintain home-cooked taste for outdoor adventures, while validating the business model through market research and analysis within 2 years.

Actions

  1. Market Research: Conducted 200+ stakeholder interviews with outdoor enthusiasts, retail shops, and industry experts to validate product-market fit.
  2. Product Development: Tested multiple preservation methods and invested in commercial freeze-drying equipment to create premium AdventurePacks that maintained taste quality.
  3. Business Planning: Developed financial forecasts, hosted packaging design competition, and pitched in three pitch competitions.

Outcome

Secured $9K across three pitch competitions, successfully defended thesis, and developed market-validated product with strong user feedback.
